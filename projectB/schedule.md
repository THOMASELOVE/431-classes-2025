## Zoom Presentations

The Zoom link for your presentation time is found on **our Shared Drive** in the **431 Project B Presentation Zoom Links** document.

1. Join the Zoom at the **Arrival Time** listed for your project. You may be placed in a waiting room. I will let you in when I am ready for you.
2. Join the Zoom from a quiet location with good internet coverage using a computer which allows you to open the camera so that I can see you, and also has a decent microphone so I can hear you easily and decent speakers so that you can hear me easily.
3. Be ready to share (as necessary) both your HTML reports (for Study 1 and Study 2) and your slides for the presentation, so you can bring either one up easily.
4. Please feel encouraged to have a pencil/pen and some paper, so you can take notes on issues I bring up.
5. If you are working with a partner, one of you will share from a single computer for both studies, even while the other one is talking.
6. If you have a partner, I will randomly determine who will present Study 1, and who will present Study 2 at the time of your presentation. Be ready to present either study at a moment's notice.
7. Before you leave the Zoom call, you will have a good understanding from me of both how you did, and what you should do to improve your work in time for the final portfolio report (due 2025-12-10 at noon.)
8. After you complete the presentation, leave my office, please remember to complete CWRU's Course Evaluation Form for 431.
